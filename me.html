welcome to my

RHEALITY ✌︎︎


Born in England and raised in Indiana, I have since dropped both accents and journeyed to the East Coast to study computer science and applied math at Harvard College. I'm fascinated by data — its vastness, its visualizations, its biases — and the endless ways we can use it. With my background in data & design, I love to work on products that make humanity happier, healthier & more equitable. In particular, I'm excited by work in machine learning, climate tech, creator tools, and education.

When I'm not braving the Bostonian winds or marveling at sunsets along the Charles, you'll find me in my cozy dining hall, poring over some code or a fun math problem, reading a book, or swapping stories with my roommates. Through stories and problems, I like identifying patterns about humans and the world.

My (almost!) four years at Harvard have been a delightful period of growth and learning. Every semester I treat classes as an opportunity During my summers, I spent time investing in climate tech companies at J.P. Morgan and quant trading at Virtu Financial, which made me fall in love with the creativity & impact I felt when using my technical skills to build bold solutions to hard problems.
