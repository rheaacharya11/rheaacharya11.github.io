WELCOME TO MY

RHEALITY ✌︎︎


Born in England and raised in Indiana, I have since dropped both accents and moved to the East Coast to study computer science and applied math at Harvard College. When I'm not braving the Bostonian winds or marveling at sunsets along the Charles, you'll find me holed up in my dining hall, poring over some code or a fun math problem, reading a book, or swapping stories with my roommates. Through stories and problems, I like identifying patterns about humans and the world.

I love to work on products that make humanity happier, healthier & more equitable. I'm fascinated by data — its vastness, its visualizations, its biases — and the endless ways we can use it. For example, the two areas I am currently most excited by are (1) how to make artificial intelligence systems safer and fairer and (2) how to use visualizations to most effectively inspire a reduction in our collective carbon footprint.
